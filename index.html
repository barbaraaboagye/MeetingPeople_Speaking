Speaking: Meeting People

Phase 1: Survival Basics

Goal: Read the frames and introduce yourself to your partner.

Step 1: Greeting & Name

"Hello. My name is ."

Step 2: Location

"I am from ."

Step 3: Age

"I am years old."

Step 4: Job

"I am a ."

Step 5: Family

"I am single/married/in a relationship/widowed."

"I have kids."

"I have brothers and sisters."

"I have a dog/no pets."

Phase 2: The Question Flip

Goal: Click the cards to see how to ask questions. Practice A/B dialogues.

"What is your name?"

Ask for Location

"Where are you from?"

Ask for a Job

"What is your job?"

Ask for Age

"How old are you?"

Ask about Family

"Are you married?"

"Do you have kids?"

"Do you have siblings?"

Phase 5: The Wingman (Level Up)

Goal: Don't introduce yourself. Introduce your partner to the class!

The Grammar Shift

  • I am 👉 He / She is
  • I have 👉 He / She has
  • My name 👉 His / Her name

The Presentation Frame

"Hello everyone. This is my friend."

"His/Her name is ."

"He/She is from ."

"He/She is a ."

"He/She has kids."

"He/She has (pets)."

Listener Reactions (Don't stay silent!)

"Oh, nice!" "Me too!" "Wow!"
